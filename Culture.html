I was born in Hong Kong and Both sides of my family are Chinese. I grew up in an average-sized Chinese family, with my mom, dad, grandpa, and grandma, and before recently moving to New Zealand, my little brother.
Snow skin mooncake is one of my favorite traditional Chinese food, it is a desert that has various flavors and it has a smooth, milky, and creamy outer skin made from pastry.
Lobster Yee Mein is my second favourite traditional Chinese food, Lobster Yee Mein is a delectable Chinese dish featuring succulent lobster paired with chewy Yee Mein noodles.
cha siu bao is my third favourite traditional Chinese food, Cha Siu Bao, also known as BBQ pork buns, is a popular Chinese dim sum delicacy. It consists of soft and fluffy steamed buns filled with savory and sweet barbecued pork.
